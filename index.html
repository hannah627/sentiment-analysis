What This Is

This is the homepage to explain each of the webtools created by Hannah Burrows as part of her University of Washington Capstone Project, with Dr. Sarah Ketchley's assistance and supervision.

There are three different webtools. They can either be used in sequence (Word Extraction Tool, Token Scoring Tool, Sentiment Analysis Tool) to create a sentiment analysis lexicon from scratch, or if you simply want to use an existing lexicon, like AFINN or our Historical Lexicon, then you can skip straight to the Sentiment Analysis Tool.

Please check back later for more information and instructions.

Created by Hannah Burrows as part of her University of Washington capstone project, in the Winter and Spring of 2024.
